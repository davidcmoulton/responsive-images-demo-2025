Resolution switching: different sizes

At different viewport widths, my image will take up different amounts of space on the page. I want the browser to pick the most appropriate image for the user's context.

        <img
          srcset="
            images/resolution-switching/resolution-switching-900x200-2x.png 900w,
            images/resolution-switching/resolution-switching-420x200-2x.png 420w
          ", 
          sizes="
            (max-width: 900px) 200px,
            900px
          "
          src="images/resolution-switching/resolution-switching-1x.png"
          alt="meaningful description" 
          alt="Meaningful alt text" 
        >
